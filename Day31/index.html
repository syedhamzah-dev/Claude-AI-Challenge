How the Control Tower works

Alerts arrive on the left as they happen across your network. Each one has a countdown — respond before it expires or it resolves itself, usually badly.

KPIs

  • Service Level — % of orders delivered on time.
  • Customer Satisfaction — how happy your customers are right now.
  • Inventory Health — how well-stocked your network is.
  • Transport Efficiency — how well your fleet & routes are running.
  • Operating Cost — cumulative spend this shift (lower is better).
  • Revenue Protected — revenue you've safeguarded through good calls.

Actions

Every alert offers a few responses. One is usually the strongest play, others are partial fixes, and "Ignore" leaves it to chance. Some consequences land immediately, others show up a few seconds later.

Difficulty

Alerts get more frequent and can stack up as the clock runs down — triage matters more late in the shift.
