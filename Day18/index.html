Summary

What this meeting was about

Leadership reviewed Q2 results, where revenue grew 12% against an 18% annual target, with the shortfall attributed to enterprise deals slipping into the next quarter (9 of 14 expected contracts closed). The group covered procurement delays and added security reviews as causes, rising marketing traffic against weaker conversion, engineering capacity constraints around an August dashboard release, a spike in support tickets tied to reporting performance, an unresolved hiring plan, and the still-unapproved annual conference budget. Several decisions were deferred pending more information, and the CEO closed by deferring public commitments on the dashboard date and scheduling follow-up for next week's leadership meeting.
