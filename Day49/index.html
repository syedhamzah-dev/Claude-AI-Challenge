What this is

This is your Personal AI Playbook — a set of reusable AI systems built around how you actually work, instead of one-off prompts you rewrite every time. Workflows are ready-made, editable prompt templates grouped into the three areas you use AI for most. The Prompt Builder lets you assemble a well-structured prompt from labelled building blocks (role, objective, context, and so on) with a live preview. The Loop Builder turns any prompt into a self-checking, self-improving one. Everything you create is saved locally in this browser only.

✳ What is the Personal AI Playbook?

In short

It's a personal library of reusable AI workflows for your Data Science journey — learning concepts, doing EDA, and job search documents — plus two builders for constructing your own prompts from scratch. Nothing here is generic: every template is written around your background and your projects, and you can edit any of it.

The four sections

Workflows
Ready-made, editable prompt templates grouped by what you use AI for. Fill in a few fields, copy the result, paste into Claude.
Prompt Builder
Assembles a single prompt from labelled blocks (role, objective, context, constraints, reasoning strategy, output format, tone, examples, quality checks) so you learn the anatomy of a strong prompt instead of memorizing one-off examples.
Loop Builder
Wraps any base task in a structure that makes the AI attempt it, self-evaluate, improve, and repeat until it meets a bar you define — useful for anything you'd otherwise iterate on manually across several messages.
My Workflows / All Workflows
Every workflow, built-in or your own, in one searchable, filterable list.
