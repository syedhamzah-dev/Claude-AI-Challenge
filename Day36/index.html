Cognitive Pattern Explorer

A quiet walk through the way you think.

This is a reflective, game-like exploration — not a test and not a diagnosis. Across three short chapters you'll respond to everyday moments, sort a few cards, and arrange your own thinking timeline. At the end, you'll get a gentle, personalized reflection on patterns that showed up along the way.

Chapter 1

Discover your thinking style

Read each moment and choose the response that feels most like you — not the one that sounds best.

Chapter 2

Choose your priorities

Drag the cards — or use the arrow buttons — so the value you lean on most is at the top, and the one you lean on least is at the bottom.
